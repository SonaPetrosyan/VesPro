

	
    In the left table are Delivery orders.

    To cancel an order, check its <Dele> box,
    then click the <Remove> button above.

    Stand in the order line to send the order to the kitchen.
    The order content will be displayed in the right table.
    Click the <Kitchen> button.

    If you need to print, click the <Print> button.

    After receiving the money, click the <Pay> button to finish.
