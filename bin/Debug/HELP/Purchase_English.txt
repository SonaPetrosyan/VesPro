
    
	Just select the desired group 
    and start working.
   
    In the left table, stand on the required line,
    set the quantity and go to the next one.
   
    if you checked the <amount> button, then write 
	the amount you paid for the product in the <amount> field.
   
    Row will appear in the right table.
   
    If you checked the box to the right of the <search> window,
    then the marker returns to the <search> field.
   
    After collecting the entire document, click the <Save> button,
    otherwise the work will have no consequence.
